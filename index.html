ABOUT ME

A results-oriented Security Operations professional with over 14 years of expertise across the maritime and aviation sectors. My career spans two of the industry's most demanding environments — luxury cruise ships and commercial aviation — giving me a uniquely comprehensive view of security in complex, high-volume, regulated settings.
